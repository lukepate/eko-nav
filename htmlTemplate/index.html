Guidelines for structure

Interactivity goes beyond simple agency and audience-grabbing gimmicks. It is a way to create a deeper bond with your audience, to keep telling classic stories, but in a way that pulls your audience further into your world and characters.

We believe the audience's desire for self expression is something we can use, but not an end in and of itself. Give them a way to react to your story - but don't forget you are the one telling it.

Understand that interactivity is another tool in your story toolkit, one that works in concert with all the others. How does having a dialog with your audience help with the pacing of your suspense? How does it sweeten the bitter pill of exposition? In what ways can it help the audience further empathize with your protagonist?
